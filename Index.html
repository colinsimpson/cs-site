About Me

I am a designer from San Diego. I consider myself a free thinker who is influenced by music and nature. As a graphic artist I enjoy communicating through imagery to deliver a concept.

My style is minimal and conceptual with an emphasis on geometry and space. I believe good design should not only make an impact, but also be usable. It should be relevant in today's society. When web banner ads take the place of poster advertisements, it is the designer's job to address alternative communications. There will always be a need for design. Creation is the purest form of action.
